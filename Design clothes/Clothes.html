How to take care of this clothes

To preserve the painting made with acrylic paints on your clothes for many years, you need to follow these simple rules:

  • The washing temperature is not higher than 40 degrees C ;
  • Moderate concentration of neutral detergents (powder) without bleach;
  • After washing, do not squeeze by twisting. Just hang it on a string and dry it;
  • When washing with your hands, do not rub the places where the painting is applied;
  • It is better to wash by turning the product inside out;
  • Dry in a straightened form;
  • Do not pre-wash (soak);
  • Iron gently and from the inside.
